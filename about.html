In all Cultures food is a vital part of being. It connects strangers, makes strangers to friends, its an environment for a common experience in families, makes us enjoy time with our friends and communities. We want to enrich your experience dining together with friends and families and provide a unique combination of dishes which you might find nowhere else. The German cuisine “Deutsche Küche” is loved all over the world and not only the Chinese have copied the famous German “Oktoberfest” and the dishes served. The German Sausage “Bratwurst” is unmatched and loved over all the world. Mexican Food is loved by 100 of Millions of people on our planet and with its flavour and healthy ingredients not only established in the Americas but touching Europe and the rest of the World. Macedonian cuisine is quite unknown but touches not only the hearts of the former Yugoslavian Community but also the Balkan with all of eastern Europe but reaches far too many communities. The founders of tasty met at a coding bootcamp where they were taking part and got the idea to create something unique. 2 of the founders are binational. All founders have moved around in different cultures and nations. The company was created in 2020 and reaches thousands of homes who want to enrich their time as a human being in a wonderful community. Aleksander, by profession a Designer with Macedonian descent is the CEO of the company who shares equal rights with Ana an iOS Developer and polylingual as the COO and Oliver as the CFO, who worked in football across the globe. Increase your happiness in enjoying our recipes. The greatest gift is to makes others happy in joining our idea and we publish your recipes with our tasty community.
